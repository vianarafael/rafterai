File, Device, and Named Pipe Actions

cron.err          /var/log/cron
auth.!=emerg    /dev/lpr1
news.=notice     |/tmp/pipe
auth-priv         root,jsmith

In the first line, all cron messages of err priority and higher are logged to the file /var/log/cron.
