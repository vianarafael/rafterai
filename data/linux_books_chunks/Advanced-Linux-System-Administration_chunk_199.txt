For a 2.4.22 kernel an additional entry in /etc/grub.conf looks like 
this:

title lvm-root

        root (hd0,1)

        kernel /vmlinuz-2.4.22 ro root=LABEL=/ 

        initrd /initrd-2.4.22-lvm.img

All we need is the initrd initrd-2.4.22-lvm.img.
