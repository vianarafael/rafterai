To see the new volume group, type the following:

# vgdisplay myvg0
  --- Volume group ---
  VG Name               myvg0
  ...
  VG Size               396.00 MiB
  PE Size               4.00 MiB
  Total PE              99
  Alloc PE / Size       0 / 0
  Free  PE / Size       99 / 396.00 MiB

4.  Of the 400MiB partition, 396MiB of space can be used in blocks of 4MiB.
