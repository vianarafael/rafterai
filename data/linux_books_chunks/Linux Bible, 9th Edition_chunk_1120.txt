 ■ CPU assignment (cpuset)—On systems with multiple CPU cores, assigns a task

 ■ Device access (devices)—Allows tasks in a cgroup to open or create (mknod)

 ■ Suspend/resume (freezer)—Suspends and resumes cgroup tasks.
