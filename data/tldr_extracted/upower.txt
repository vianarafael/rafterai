# upower


- Display power and battery information:

`upower {{[-d|--dump]}}`

- List all power devices:

`upower {{[-e|--enumerate]}}`

- Watch for and print power status changes:

`upower {{[-m|--monitor]}}`

- Watch for and print detailed power status changes:

`upower --monitor-detail`

- Display version:

`upower {{[-v|--version]}}`
