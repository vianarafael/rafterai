# nmcli radio


- Show status of Wi-Fi:

`nmcli radio wifi`

- Turn Wi-Fi on or off:

`nmcli radio wifi {{on|off}}`

- Show status of WWAN:

`nmcli radio wwan`

- Turn WWAN on or off:

`nmcli radio wwan {{on|off}}`

- Show status of both switches:

`nmcli radio all`

- Turn both switches on or off:

`nmcli radio all {{on|off}}`
