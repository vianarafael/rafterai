# plasmashell


- Restart `plasmashell`:

`systemctl restart --user plasma-plasmashell`

- Restart `plasmashell` without systemd:

`plasmashell --replace & disown`

- Display help on command-line options:

`plasmashell {{[-h|--help]}}`

- Display help, including Qt options:

`plasmashell --help-all`
